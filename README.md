# Electronics Sales Performance Analysis — FY 2024
Tool: Microsoft Excel + Power Query
Dataset: 295 transactions | 12 months | 4 regions | 6 products

## Key Findings
- Nov–Dec drove 24% of annual revenue (festive season effect)
- Earphones: highest profit margin at 45% — hidden gem product
- East region underperforming — 20% revenue but 25% order volume
- Top 2 salespeople generate 35%+ of total revenue
